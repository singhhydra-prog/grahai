# Skill: content-pipeline

## Trigger
Use this skill when working on SEO content, blog posts, social media content, marketing automation, or organic growth strategies for GrahAI.

## Outcome
Published content (blog posts, social media, email sequences) that drives organic traffic to GrahAI.

## Strategy (from Feasibility Study)
- AI agents produce 70-80% of content; founder acts as editor-in-chief
- Target: 2-3 SEO-optimized posts/week + 5-10 social posts/week
- Long-tail Vedic content (e.g., "Mars in 7th House for Taurus Lagna" articles)
- Repurpose across: blog → Instagram reels → YouTube shorts → WhatsApp broadcasts

## Content Types
1. **SEO blog posts**: Long-tail Vedic astrology combinations (e.g., "Saturn in 10th house career effects")
2. **Daily horoscope posts**: Auto-generated from insight engine, posted to social
3. **Educational content**: "What is Mangal Dosha?", "Understanding Vimshottari Dasha"
4. **Seasonal content**: Wedding season compatibility, exam season career guidance, New Year predictions

## Steps
1. **Keyword research**: Use AI to find high-volume, low-competition Vedic astrology keywords
2. **Outline**: Generate article outline with H2/H3 structure targeting the keyword
3. **Draft**: Write the article using GrahAI's astrology knowledge base for accuracy
4. **Edit**: Human review for tone, accuracy, and brand voice
5. **Publish**: Post to blog/CMS
6. **Repurpose**: Create social media snippets, email excerpts, and short-form video scripts

## Brand Voice Rules
- Position AI as "instant, practical guidance" — NOT a guru replacement
- Use clear, modern language — avoid excessive Sanskrit jargon
- Always ground claims in chart data or classical references
- Include disclaimers: "For entertainment and guidance purposes"
- Never make medical, legal, or financial claims
