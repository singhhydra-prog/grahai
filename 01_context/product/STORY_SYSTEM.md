# GrahAI Story System

> Life is not a pile of disconnected facts. GrahAI tells the user their story.

---

## Why Story Matters

People don't come to astrology for data. They come because they're trying to make sense of their life — why things feel the way they do, what's changing, and where it's heading. Data without narrative is noise. GrahAI's job is to turn chart data into a coherent life story.

This doesn't mean being poetic or vague. It means being structured. A good story has chapters, themes, tensions, and resolution arcs. A birth chart has all of these. GrahAI's story system maps the chart's structural elements to a narrative framework the user can feel and follow.

---

## The Story Framework

Every user's chart tells a story with these components:

### 1. Current Chapter

**What it is:** The Mahadasha + Antardasha period defines the current "chapter" of the user's life. This is the broadest narrative frame.

**How to present it:**
> "You're in your [Mahadasha Lord] chapter — a period of [core theme] that runs from [start] to [end]. Within that, your current [Antardasha Lord] sub-chapter (until [date]) brings [sub-theme] into focus."

**Example:**
> "You're in your Venus Mahadasha — a chapter of relationships, beauty, and resources that runs from 2021 to 2041. Within that, your current Mars Antardasha (until August 2027) brings energy, ambition, and some friction into your relationships and creative pursuits."

**Chart factors:** Mahadasha lord + its house placement + dignity + the houses it rules. Antardasha lord with the same analysis.

**Rules:**
- Always name the chapter in daily insights and chat answers
- Frame it as a lens, not a sentence: "During this chapter, everything you experience is filtered through Venus themes"
- Note when a chapter is about to change (within 6 months): "Your Mars sub-period ends in August — prepare for a shift toward..."

### 2. Recurring Life Themes

**What it is:** The yogas, strong houses, and dominant planets that define the user's life regardless of timing. These are the through-lines of their story.

**How to identify:**
- **Strongest planets** (by Shadbala or dignity): These define core character themes
- **Active yogas:** Gaja Kesari (wisdom/respect), Dhana (wealth), Raja (authority), etc.
- **Dominant houses** (by Ashtakavarga score or multiple planet occupancy): These life areas are always prominent

**How to present it:**
> "Your chart's recurring themes are [theme 1] and [theme 2]. These show up across different periods of your life — in different forms, but always recognizable."

**Example:**
> "Two themes keep recurring in your chart: the drive to build something tangible (Saturn strong in your 10th, Mars in your 2nd) and a deep inner world that doesn't always match the outer one (Moon in 12th, Ketu conjunct ascendant). You may notice that your greatest achievements often come with a feeling of quiet solitude."

**Rules:**
- Identify 2-3 recurring themes maximum (more becomes unfocused)
- Reference these themes across different outputs to create continuity
- When a daily insight or report touches a recurring theme, name it: "This connects to one of your chart's enduring patterns..."

### 3. Emotional Pattern

**What it is:** How the user processes emotions, what triggers them, and what soothes them. Primarily derived from Moon placement.

**Chart factors:**
- Moon sign + house + dignity → emotional baseline
- Moon's nakshatra → instinctive emotional style
- 4th house condition → inner security and comfort needs
- Moon's aspects and conjunctions → what colors the emotional life
- Current transit to natal Moon → what's stirring emotions now

**How to present it:**
> "Your emotional pattern — how you naturally process what you feel — is shaped by your Moon in [sign] in your [house]. This means [plain description of emotional style]."

**Example:**
> "Your emotional pattern is deeply private and intense. Moon in Scorpio in the 8th house means you feel things at full depth, but rarely show it. You process through solitude, research, or creative intensity — not through talking it out. When someone breaks your trust, the wound goes deep. When someone earns it, you're loyal without limit."

**Rules:**
- Present emotional patterns in chat answers about relationships, stress, and self-understanding
- In daily insights, reference how today's energy interacts with the user's emotional pattern
- Never pathologize: "deep emotions" not "emotional problems"

### 4. Timing Pattern

**What it is:** The rhythm of expansion and contraction, activity and rest, opportunity and challenge in the user's life. Derived from Dasha sequence and major transits.

**How to present it:**
> "Your life has a rhythm. Right now you're in a [expansion/contraction/transition] phase. This started around [date] and shifts around [date]."

**Chart factors:**
- Dasha sequence → which planet's themes are activated when
- Saturn transit (Sade Sati, return, etc.) → structural life shifts
- Jupiter transit → growth windows
- Rahu/Ketu transit → disruption/karma windows

**Example:**
> "You're in an expansionary phase right now. Jupiter transiting your 1st house (until May 2027) + Venus Mahadasha = a period where life opens up — opportunities arrive, relationships deepen, and your sense of self expands. This doesn't mean everything is easy, but the overall arc is growth. Contrast this with 2019-2022, when Saturn crossed your 12th house — a period of endings and withdrawal that was preparing you for this opening."

**Rules:**
- In annual forecasts, map the timing pattern across the full year
- In daily insights, locate "today" within the larger timing arc
- Name past phases to create historical narrative ("after your challenging Saturn period...")

### 5. Relationship Pattern

**What it is:** How the user connects with others — what they seek, what challenges them, and what their chart says about partnership dynamics.

**Chart factors:**
- 7th house + lord → partnership style
- Venus sign + house + dignity → love language
- D9 Navamsa → deeper marriage/commitment potential
- Current dasha/transit to 7th → timing of relationship events

**How to present it:**
> "Your relationship pattern — what you seek and what challenges you in partnerships — is shaped by [7th house factors]. You're drawn to [partner archetype] and your growth edge in relationships is [challenge]."

**Rules:**
- Use in love reports, compatibility readings, and relationship-themed chat answers
- Connect relationship pattern to current chapter: "During this Venus Mahadasha, relationships are center stage..."
- Be honest about challenges without being discouraging

### 6. Career Pattern

**What it is:** The user's professional DNA — what work suits them, how they achieve, and where their career energy flows.

**Chart factors:**
- 10th house + lord → career archetype
- D10 Dasamsa → career depth chart
- Saturn placement → discipline and structure
- Mercury/Jupiter → intellectual and advisory capacity
- Current dasha/transit to 10th → career timing

**How to present it:**
> "Your career pattern shows [archetype]. You thrive when [working condition]. Your current chapter suggests [timing note about career]."

### 7. What Is Changing Now

**What it is:** The most active shift in the user's chart. What's in flux, what's being tested, what's emerging.

**How to identify:** Look for:
- Recent dasha change (within last 6 months)
- Major transit activating a natal planet (Saturn, Jupiter, or Rahu/Ketu conjunct or opposing)
- Eclipse season touching natal planets or angles

**How to present it:**
> "What's actively changing for you right now: [specific shift]. This started around [date] and continues through [date]. It's asking you to [growth direction]."

**Example:**
> "What's changing now: Saturn entered your 7th house in January, and won't leave until 2028. This is a period where relationships get serious — casual connections fall away, committed ones are tested and strengthened. If you've been avoiding a difficult conversation in a partnership, this transit makes it unavoidable. The growth direction is commitment and honesty."

### 8. What This Phase Is Teaching

**What it is:** The meta-lesson of the current astrological period. Not what's happening, but what it means.

**How to present it:**
> "This phase of your life is teaching you [lesson]. You might notice [pattern] showing up in multiple areas. The invitation is [growth direction]."

**Example:**
> "This phase is teaching you to trust your own authority. With Saturn in your 10th (career) and Rahu in your 1st (identity), multiple forces are pushing you to stop deferring and start leading. You might notice this showing up at work, in family dynamics, and even in how you make daily decisions. The invitation is to step forward, even when it feels uncomfortable."

**Rules:**
- Keep this high-level — one sentence for the lesson, one for the evidence, one for the invitation
- Use at the end of reports, in weekly guidance, and in long-form chat answers
- This should feel wise, not preachy. It's an observation, not an instruction.

## Story Continuity Across Surfaces

### The Narrative Thread

The user's story should feel consistent across every surface they touch. The current chapter named in the daily insight should be the same chapter referenced in the report. The emotional pattern described in a chat answer should echo what was written in the compatibility analysis.

**Implementation:**
- Generate a **Chart Story Snapshot** object once during onboarding (or on first meaningful interaction). Store it in the user's profile or local cache.
- The snapshot contains:
  ```
  {
    currentChapter: { mahadasha, antardasha, theme, endDate },
    recurringThemes: [{ theme, evidence }],
    emotionalPattern: { moonSign, moonHouse, style },
    timingPhase: { type: 'expansion' | 'contraction' | 'transition', until },
    activeChange: { factor, meaning, until },
    lesson: string
  }
  ```
- Every output generator (daily, chat, report, compatibility) receives this snapshot and references it

### Cross-Surface References

| When the user is on... | Reference their... |
|------------------------|-------------------|
| Home (daily insight) | Current chapter + timing phase + what's changing |
| Ask (chat answer) | Current chapter + emotional pattern + relevant theme |
| Reports | All story elements relevant to the report's topic |
| Compatibility | Relationship pattern + emotional pattern + current chapter |
| Profile | Full story summary as "Your Astrological Identity" |

### Avoiding Repetition

The same story elements should appear across surfaces, but worded differently each time:

- **Home:** "During your Venus chapter, today's Mercury transit sharpens communication about money..."
- **Chat:** "You asked about finances. With Venus as your current Mahadasha lord and Mercury now transiting your 2nd house..."
- **Report:** "Your wealth patterns are shaped by Venus's dominance in your current life phase, combined with Mercury's influence on your 2nd house..."

Same narrative thread, different entry points, different depth levels.

## Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Why It Fails | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| "The universe is sending you a message" | Vague, no chart basis, sounds like fortune cookie | Name the specific chart factor creating the pattern |
| "This is a time of transformation" | Could apply to anyone at any time | Specify WHAT is transforming and WHY (which transit, which dasha) |
| "Trust the journey" | Empty encouragement without substance | Name what the journey IS — "This Saturn transit is asking you to build discipline in [area]" |
| Listing 10 separate astrological factors without connecting them | Data dump, not a story | Pick the 2-3 most relevant factors and weave them into one narrative |
| "In your past life..." | Unfounded, not in the system's scope | Stick to what's in the chart. Karmic themes through Rahu/Ketu are fine; "past life" specifics are not |

## Implementation Notes

### New Data Object: ChartStorySnapshot
- Compute once after birth chart calculation
- Store in `profiles` table or generate on-the-fly from cached chart data
- Pass to all output generators as a context parameter
- Update when Antardasha changes (check on each session start)

### Generator Changes
- Each report generator receives `storySnapshot` alongside `reportData`
- The opening summary of every report must reference `currentChapter`
- At least one section must reference `recurringThemes` or `lesson`
- The remedies section must reference `activeChange`

### Prompt Changes (AI Chat)
- System prompt includes the user's `storySnapshot` as structured data
- Instruction: "Always reference the user's current chapter and at least one recurring theme in your response"
- Instruction: "Connect your answer to the larger story of what this phase is teaching the user"

### Daily Insight Changes
- Cron generator receives the user's `storySnapshot`
- Opening sentence connects today's transit to `currentChapter`
- Closing takeaway references `lesson` or `timingPhase`
