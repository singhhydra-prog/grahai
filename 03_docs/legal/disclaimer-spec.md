# Astrology Disclaimer Specification — GrahAI

## Overview
**CRITICAL LEGAL REQUIREMENT:** Prominent disclaimers must appear on all report pages and checkout. Establishes that GrahAI readings are for entertainment/guidance only, not professional advice. Protects GrahAI from liability claims; protects users from mistaking astrology for certified expertise. Non-negotiable compliance component.

## Primary Disclaimer (Homepage & Report Pages)

### Display Requirement
- **Visibility:** Must appear ABOVE report content (top of page)
- **Size:** Minimum 12pt font; readable on mobile (not tiny print)
- **Color Contrast:** High contrast (gold/yellow on dark background); meets WCAG accessibility standards
- **Persistence:** Always visible; not dismissible or collapsible
- **Placement:** On PrivacyPolicyPage.tsx, report generation pages, checkout

### Text Version (Primary Disclaimer)

```
⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

GrahAI readings are generated by artificial intelligence and are for entertainment,
cultural exploration, and personal reflection purposes only.

These readings are NOT professional advice and should NOT be used to make
decisions regarding:
• Medical or health matters → Consult a licensed physician
• Legal issues → Consult a qualified attorney
• Financial or investment decisions → Consult a financial advisor
• Mental health or psychological concerns → Consult a mental health professional

Astrology readings do NOT guarantee outcomes. Your future is not predetermined.
You retain complete free will and responsibility for all decisions and their
consequences.

By using GrahAI, you acknowledge you understand this disclaimer and use readings
at your own risk.
```

GrahAI integrates AI to democratize astrological insights, making them
