## Category 1: Account Management (6 Q&As)

### Q1: How do I sign up for GrahAI?
**A:**
1. Visit grahai-app.vercel.app
2. Click "Sign Up" (top-right or homepage)
3. Enter email and create password (8+ characters, mix of letters/numbers)
4. Verify email: Check inbox for verification link; click to confirm
5. Complete profile: Enter full name and accept Terms of Service
6. You're ready to generate your first reading!

**Tip:** Use a personal email you regularly check (Gmail, Outlook). Corporate emails may block OTP codes.

---

### Q2: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
**A:**
1. Go to login page (grahai-app.vercel.app/login)
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter email address associated with account
4. Check inbox for password reset link (valid for 1 hour)
5. Click link and create new password
6. Log in with new password

**Still not working?** Check spam folder. If still unresolved, email support@grahai.com.

---

### Q3: What is OTP and why do I need it?
**A:**
OTP = One-Time Password. It's a 6-digit code sent to your email or phone for verification.

**When you'll see OTP:**
- First login from new device
- Sensitive account changes (email update, payment)
- Optional 2-factor authentication (if you enable it)

**To receive OTP:**
- Check email (primary method)
- Or phone (if you provided phone number and enabled SMS OTP)
- OTP valid for 10 minutes; request new one if expired

**Why?** OTP adds security layer; prevents unauthorized account access.

---

### Q4: Can I change my email or phone number?
**A:**
Yes. Go to Account Settings > Profile.

**To update email:**
1. Click "Edit" next to email
2. Enter new email
3. Verify new email via confirmation link sent to that address
4. Old email no longer works for login; use new email instead

**To update phone:**
1. Click "Edit" next to phone number
2. Enter new number
3. Verify via OTP sent to that number
4. Update complete

**Note:** Birth data (name, DOB, location, time) cannot be changed after reports generated (prevents cheating). Contact support@grahai.com if major correction needed.

---

### Q5: How do I delete my account and data?
**A:**
1. Go to Account Settings > Privacy & Security
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Click "Request Account Deletion"
4. Confirm via email link (valid 24 hours)
5. Account deleted permanently within 24 hours

**What happens:**
- ✓ Account inaccessible; cannot re-login
- ✓ All reports archived (you cannot view old reports)
- ✓ Birth data permanently anonymized
- ✗ Cannot recover account after deletion

**Note:** You have 30 days to request permanent data deletion (per privacy law). GrahAI complies with all deletion requests.

---

### Q6: Can I have multiple accounts?
**A:**
No. GrahAI allows only one active account per email address.

**Why?** To prevent abuse (multiple free trials, refund fraud, data duplication).

**If you have old account, you can:**
- Delete old account, then use email to create new account
- Contact support@grahai.com to merge accounts (limited circumstances)

## Category 2: Reports & Astrology (7 Q&As)

### Q7: How are astrology reports generated?
**A:**
GrahAI uses AI trained on Vedic astrology principles:

1. **Birth Data Input:** You provide date, time, location of birth
2. **Chart Calculation:** AI calculates planetary positions, houses, aspects
3. **Interpretation:** AI generates personalized text analysis
4. **Report Formatting:** PDF created with charts, interpretations, guidance
5. **Delivery:** Report emailed and saved in your account

**Human Involvement:** None. Reports are 100% AI-generated. While accurate for most users, interpretations may differ from human astrologers.

**Time:** Report generated within 1-5 minutes of payment.

---

### Q8: How accurate are the astrology reports?
**A:**
**Accuracy depends on:**
1. **Birth Data Accuracy:** If time is incorrect, chart is incorrect (15 min = big difference)
2. **Birth Time Source:** Hospital records most reliable; family recollection less reliable
3. **Astrology Itself:** Vedic astrology is interpretive (not provable science)
4. **AI Interpretation:** AI follows traditional rules; some humans may interpret differently

**Reality Check:**
- Astrological predictions are NOT scientifically proven
- Different astrologers interpret same chart differently
- Charts show tendencies, not certainties
- Outcomes depend heavily on your actions and choices

**If uncertain about accuracy:**
- Verify birth time with birth certificate or hospital
- Consult a human Vedic astrologer for comparison
- Use readings for reflection, not decision-making

### Q12: What if my birth time is unknown or approximate?
**A:**
If birth time is unknown:

**Report Quality:** Less precise. House placements (which planet rules life areas) depend on birth time. Inaccurate time = inaccurate houses.

**Options:**
1. **Find Exact Time:** Check hospital records, birth certificate, or family records
2. **Best Guess:** If time must be approximate, use your best estimate
3. **Sunrise Chart:** Astrologers sometimes use sunrise if time truly unknown; GrahAI doesn't offer this
4. **House-Free Report:** Use report as general guide; weight non-house interpretations more heavily

**Recommendation:** Spend time finding exact time (within ±5 minutes) for accurate reading.

### Q18: Why does my credit card charge show as "Razorpay" not "GrahAI"?
**A:**
Payments processed through Razorpay (payment processor), not directly by GrahAI.

**Why:** Security. Razorpay handles sensitive card data; GrahAI never stores card information. Your card is charged by Razorpay; they transfer amount to GrahAI.

**Verification:**
- Statement shows: "RAZORPAY PAYMENT" or similar
- Your invoice email from GrahAI shows details
- Order ID matches between invoice and bank statement

**Concern?** Contact support@grahai.com to verify transaction; they can cross-check with Razorpay.

### Q23: The page is slow or not loading. What should I do?
**A:**
**Quick Fixes:**
1. **Refresh Page:** Press Ctrl+R (Windows) or Cmd+R (Mac)
2. **Clear Browser Cache:** Settings > Clear browsing data; check "Cached images and files"
3. **Disable Extensions:** Ad blockers, VPNs can slow page load; try disabling temporarily
4. **Check Internet:** Ensure you have stable WiFi or mobile connection
5. **Try Different Browser:** If issue persists, try Chrome or Firefox

**If still slow:**
1. **Check GrahAI Status:** Visit https://status.grahai.app (if available)
2. **Wait & Retry:** If server is down, try again in 15 minutes
3. **Contact Support:** Email support@grahai.com with:
   - Browser and device type
   - Screenshot of error (if any)
   - Time issue occurred

**Known Issues:**
- Payment page may take 2-3 seconds to load (Razorpay integration)
- PDF download may take 10-15 seconds (large file size)
